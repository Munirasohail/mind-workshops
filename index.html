Founder & CEO

Meet Munira Sohail

Munira Sohail is the Founder & CEO of MIND – Mathematical Insight, Numeracy & Development. For more than three decades, mathematics has been her passion, purpose and lifelong area of exploration.

She holds a degree in Civil Engineering from NED University of Engineering & Technology, where she graduated with distinction. She completed her MBA from Hamdard Institute of Management Sciences, earning two Gold Medals, and recently completed her MS in Mathematics from IBA Karachi, securing Third Position.

Alongside her educational work, she serves as a Partner and Chief Marketing Officer in her family business and actively volunteers as an educational leader within the Aga Khan community.

Her teaching philosophy connects mathematics with everyday life, the universe and the beauty of God's creation. Her mission is to replace fear and memorization with curiosity, confidence and deep conceptual understanding.

“I do not just teach mathematics. I help people experience it.”
